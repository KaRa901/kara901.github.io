About me

Kara is a passionate data analyst and data scientist, currently resident in Berlin. Her special interest are foundational questions about the representative function of (scientific) models. How can we represent complex phenomena the best way? How much should we care about isomorphic similarity between target and model? Is it allowed to conceptualize and generalize patterns in big data or are they more like a status quo description of complex phenomena with a maximum variability over time? How much does modeling depends on human factor like creativity and the work environment?

While working in an analytical role with data analysis and ML tools, she cares a lot about this philosophical questions and loves to integrate them in her future work.
