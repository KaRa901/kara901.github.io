Kara Riebesel (*1988, Germany) Kara is an interdisciplinary thinker with a background in science, film, and data. She is currently pursuing a master’s in Theorie und Geschichte der Wissenschaft und Technik at TU Berlin, focusing on transdisciplinary aspects of physics and aesthetics in science. She has worked in film production and screenwriting while also building a career in data analytics and finance. Her ability to recognize patterns across fields helps her develop creative and structured solutions. In her artistic work, she explores queer utopian ideas and personal experiences. She is also interested in interdisciplinary futures in the sciences and seeks new narratives at the intersection of technology, humanity, and nature. As a trans* feminine and queer advocate, she co-leads an LGBTQI*+ self-help group using Psychodrama methods and works to create inclusive and diverse professional environments.
